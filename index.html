INFLUENTIAL PERSON

Ever since I was old enough and finally had my mother's blessing to listen to hip-hop, I instantly felt captivated by Nasir Jones, story telling. In a world thousands of miles away from the Rocky Mountains, I was introduce to another part of America that I could only visit visually by watching movies. Back then there was no you tube, or smarphones, to see first hand the realities of what other parts of the country were like. While I don't condone gun violence, or inner city violence, listening to the stories of Nas in my youth allowed me to understand the struggles of other communities, and in mist of all of this I was able to fall in the depths of appreciation for storytelling and hip-hop. With this page I pay tribute to one of the greatest Rappers, becuase they don't make rappers like they used to.
